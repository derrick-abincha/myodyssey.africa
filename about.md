Odyssey Africa is a dedicated arm of **Odyssey**, the immigration compliance platform built for global teams operating across Africa.

We combine **cutting-edge automation** with a trusted network of **local immigration lawyers and compliance experts** in 15+ African countries to help companies:

- Secure work permits and visas faster
- Stay compliant with evolving local regulations
- Reduce administrative overhead and legal risk

Whether you’re scaling a remote team in Nigeria, opening an entity in Kenya, or relocating talent to South Africa — **we handle the complexity so you can focus on growth**.

Our mission: **Make cross-border work in Africa seamless, predictable, and human-centered.**
